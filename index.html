Adding a task:

  1. Fill the fields with the requested data
  2. Press enter or hit the plus button on the right top corner

Updating a task:

  1. Hit the edit button on the right bottom corner of the task you want to change
  2. Change the field you want modify
  3. Press enter or hit the check button on the right top corner

Deleting a task:

  1. Hit the X button on the right top corner of the task you wish to delete
